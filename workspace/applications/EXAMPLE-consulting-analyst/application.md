## Full JD Text

**Business Analyst — Strategy & Operations | Meridian & Co.**

Meridian & Co. is a management consulting firm partnering with leading organizations to solve their hardest
strategic and operational problems. Our Business Analysts join client teams from day one, owning workstreams
and turning ambiguous problems into structured, defensible recommendations.

**What you'll do**
- Own a workstream on a client engagement: structure the problem, gather and analyze data, and build the
  analysis that supports the team's recommendation.
- Conduct research and interviews — market sizing, competitive teardowns, customer and stakeholder interviews —
  and synthesize findings into clear, client-ready outputs.
- Build the quantitative backbone: financial models, profitability and market analyses, and the "so what" that
  turns numbers into a decision.
- Communicate clearly: distill complex analysis into crisp slides and a recommendation a partner can take to
  the client.
- Work alongside consultants, managers, and client stakeholders; manage your own deadlines in a fast-paced,
  high-ownership environment.

**What we look for**
- Bachelor's degree (any discipline); strong academic record.
- Structured, hypothesis-driven problem solving (MECE, issue trees, first-principles).
- Comfort with quantitative analysis and building a model from scratch.
- Excellent written and verbal communication; presence under pressure.
- Curiosity about emerging technology, including AI, and how it changes how consultants work.
- Willingness to travel based on client needs.
