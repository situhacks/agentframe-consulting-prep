# Voice — Priya Raman

- Direct and plain. No corporate filler ("synergize", "leverage", "passionate about").
- Short sentences, active voice. Lead with the point.
- No em-dashes for drama; a clause is fine, not a dramatic reveal.
- Never open a cover letter with "I am writing to express my interest in..." — open with a real hook.
- Confident, not boastful. Show the work, let the result speak.
- A little warmth is fine; no jokes that try too hard.
