# Financial Services - Consumer Banking

> Industry prior for case practice - use it to ask sharper questions and sanity-check, NOT as case facts. Solve
> the case with its own data. (From the SFU MCCP industry snapshots.)

**Overview.** Deposits, credit cards, consumer loans, payments. Collect from savers, lend to borrowers.

**Key trends.**
- Consolidated, mature - growth mainly via acquisition
- Aging boomers create a big retirement-product market
- Offshoring back-office to cut cost
- Online banking

**Competitive landscape.**
- Large national players vs. regional banks/credit unions
- Biggest players extend into IB, securitization, trading - increasingly opaque

**Customers.**
- Individuals
- High-net-worth (priority segment)
- SMBs below IB-financing size

**Channels.**
- Branches & tellers (still large)
- ATM / online banking growing
- Credit cards, home loans to grow asset base

**Profit model.** Revenue: net interest spread (borrow cost vs. lending rate) + fees. Cost: overhead (branches, admin, compliance), salaries, bad-debt expense.
