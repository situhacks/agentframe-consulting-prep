# Private Equity (PE)

> Industry prior for case practice - use it to ask sharper questions and sanity-check, NOT as case facts. Solve
> the case with its own data. (From the SFU MCCP industry snapshots.)

**Overview.** Equity not publicly traded: LBOs, venture capital, mezzanine, distressed, growth capital.

**Key trends.**
- Larger equity per deal
- Possible wave of deal failures coming
- Secondary trading of commitments rising
- Growing need for cash margins

**Competitive landscape.**
- Large (>$5B), Mid ($250M-$5B), Small (<$250M)
- Customers from small family firms to large corporates

**Customers.**
- Institutional investors
- Portfolio companies (the targets)

**Channels.**
- LBO: control via high borrowing
- VC: cash for shares (startups)
- Mezzanine: equity options + sub debt
- Growth capital; Distressed investing

**Profit model.** Value creation: financial levers (capital structure, access to cash) + operational levers (efficiency, new mgmt). Decision lens: required ROI, timeframe, is there a better use of the money.
