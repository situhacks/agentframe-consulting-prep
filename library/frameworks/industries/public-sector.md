# Public Sector (Government - Canada)

> Industry prior for case practice - use it to ask sharper questions and sanity-check, NOT as case facts. Solve
> the case with its own data. (From the SFU MCCP industry snapshots.)

**Overview.** People, systems and processes delivering government services (federal/provincial/municipal) to the public.

**Key trends.**
- Tech/data/analytics to raise productivity & outcomes
- Outsourcing + outcome/performance-based contracting
- Long-term planning, larger integrated contracts, cutting inefficient services
- Demand for accountability & fiscal efficiency (media scrutiny)
- Health care growing (aging population)

**Competitive landscape.**
- Not applicable - no competitive market in the usual sense

**Customers.**
- Industry associations
- Businesses (small/medium/large)
- Citizens

**Channels.**
- Online (growing)
- Municipal/regional/national offices

**Profit model.** Funded by: tax collection (income/corporate/sales/property/fuel/tobacco), natural resources (Crown land), user fees & investments (MSP), federal transfers, Crown corporations. 'Profit' = efficiency & outcomes, not margin.
