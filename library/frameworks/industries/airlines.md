# Airlines

> Industry prior for case practice - use it to ask sharper questions and sanity-check, NOT as case facts. Solve
> the case with its own data. (From the SFU MCCP industry snapshots.)

**Overview.** Air transport for passengers and/or freight.

**Key trends.**
- Consolidation
- Fare competition
- Rise of low-cost carriers

**Competitive landscape.**
- Legacy airlines vs. low-cost carriers on domestic routes
- Price is the major competitive factor

**Customers.**
- Individuals
- Corporations / small businesses
- Travel sites & resellers (agents)

**Channels.**
- Internet
- Telephone
- Travel agents
- Over-the-counter

**Profit model.** Revenue: tickets, baggage fees, food & beverage. VC: fuel, F&B, hourly ground crew. FC: aircraft leases, airport gate leases, IT/admin, salaried staff (pilots).
