# Telecommunications

> Industry prior for case practice - use it to ask sharper questions and sanity-check, NOT as case facts. Solve
> the case with its own data. (From the SFU MCCP industry snapshots.)

**Overview.** Hardware (satellites, fibre, phones), services (switching, mobile/internet access, private networks), and the software that runs it.

**Key trends.**
- Grown/evolved fast for 20 years
- Smartphone penetration >50% globally
- Households dropping landlines for mobile/VoIP (Skype, Hangouts)
- Wave of wireless-carrier consolidation
- Cable companies entering wireless

**Competitive landscape.**
- Very competitive; several large players
- Cable cos. chasing wireless customers

**Customers.**
- Individuals
- Corporations
- Governments

**Channels.**
- Carrier-owned stores + retailers (Best Buy, Walmart, London Drugs)
- Carrier online stores (Bell, Rogers, Telus) + Amazon

**Profit model.** Revenue: subscriptions, data (SMS/email/internet), mobile advertising, app stores. Cost: VC marketing & salaries; FC capital (cell towers, network maintenance, stores), overhead.
