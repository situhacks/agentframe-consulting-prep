# Retail

> Industry prior for case practice - use it to ask sharper questions and sanity-check, NOT as case facts. Solve
> the case with its own data. (From the SFU MCCP industry snapshots.)

**Overview.** Goods/services/systems/people across the value chain culminating in selling to consumers through various channels. Profit-driven.

**Key trends.**
- Convergence & diversification to differentiate & grow
- Digital/social/mobile changing how consumers shop
- Rising consumer spend (value & frequency), helped by low oil prices
- Highly competitive

**Competitive landscape.**
- Highly competitive market
- B2B and B2C players

**Customers.**
- B2B
- B2C

**Channels.**
- Brick-and-mortar
- Online

**Profit model.** Revenue: units sold x price per unit. Cost: COGS, distribution, selling & admin, fixed costs (rent, utilities, salaries, depreciation, interest).
