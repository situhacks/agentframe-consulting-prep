# Tourism

> Industry prior for case practice - use it to ask sharper questions and sanity-check, NOT as case facts. Solve
> the case with its own data. (From the SFU MCCP industry snapshots.)

**Overview.** Travel for pleasure; the business of attracting, accommodating and entertaining tourists. Major income source for many countries (~10% of global GDP).

**Key trends.**
- Major disruption via the sharing economy
- Resilient globally despite slow growth / geopolitics
- Chinese outbound travel growing (~$500B in 2014)
- Millennials seeking adventure
- Emerging: space tourism

**Competitive landscape.**
- Competitive: agencies, hotels, Airbnb, service providers vie via technology
- Forward integration by suppliers (packaging); agencies can't buy out airlines

**Customers.**
- Individuals
- Corporations

**Channels.**
- Independently or via a service provider (travel agent)
- Online - platforms ease access & planning

**Profit model.** Revenue: transport (cruises, flights) + accommodation (hotels, rentals) services. Cost: VC marketing & salaries; FC capital (equipment, accommodation, insurance).
