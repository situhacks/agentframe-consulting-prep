# Professional Services

> Industry prior for case practice - use it to ask sharper questions and sanity-check, NOT as case facts. Solve
> the case with its own data. (From the SFU MCCP industry snapshots.)

**Overview.** Tertiary-sector work needing special training/licenses (architects, auditors, engineers, doctors, lawyers) or specialist business support (tax, accounting, IT, management advice).

**Key trends.**
- ~10% growth in 2015
- Disruption via automation & predictive analytics
- Clients getting smarter, more collaborative
- Very competitive

**Competitive landscape.**
- Several large players
- Multinationals (Deloitte, PwC) bought consulting firms (Monitor, Booz)

**Customers.**
- Corporations (F100/1000/5000, SMEs)
- Governments
- Individuals
- B2B

**Channels.**
- B2B engagements

**Profit model.** Revenue: engagements - lump-sum contracts or billable rate x hours + overhead (travel, disbursements). Cost: overhead.
