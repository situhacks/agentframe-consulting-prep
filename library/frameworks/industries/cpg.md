# Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG)

> Industry prior for case practice - use it to ask sharper questions and sanity-check, NOT as case facts. Solve
> the case with its own data. (From the SFU MCCP industry snapshots.)

**Overview.** Household products sold to consumers.

**Key trends.**
- Lifestyle/behaviour shifts (aging, social, go-green, downturns)
- New products critical (new, improved, line extensions)
- Product-mix & brand management central
- Emerging markets = future growth

**Competitive landscape.**
- P&G, Unilever, L'Oreal, Kimberly-Clark, Colgate, J&J
- Frito Lay, PepsiCo, Nestle

**Customers.**
- Individuals
- Discount wholesalers (Costco)
- Big-box (Walmart)
- Convenience (Shoppers)

**Channels.**
- Retail
- Wholesale
- Direct (web/mail)

**Profit model.** Revenue: volume of goods sold + price premium on branded goods. Cost: branding, S&M, COGS (commodity raw + packaging).
