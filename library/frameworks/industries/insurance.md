# Financial Services - Insurance

> Industry prior for case practice - use it to ask sharper questions and sanity-check, NOT as case facts. Solve
> the case with its own data. (From the SFU MCCP industry snapshots.)

**Overview.** Underwriting risk: customers pay premiums; insurer invests the float to match future liabilities.

**Key trends.**
- Credit crises threaten solvency (illiquid, hard-to-value assets)
- Healthcare policy shifts reshape health insurance
- Focus on managing risk & controlling cost

**Competitive landscape.**
- Large integrated players across segments
- Some niche/segment players
- Crown corporations

**Customers.**
- Individuals managing home/auto risk
- SMB/large businesses managing property/liability risk

**Channels.**
- Insurance agents (sales force) still own much of front-end
- Online sales growing

**Profit model.** Revenue: spread between premiums collected and claims paid over time (plus investment income on the float). Cost: overhead (admin, compliance), salaries, sales commissions, marketing.
